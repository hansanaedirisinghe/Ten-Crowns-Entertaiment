Born From Legend

At TEN CROWNS ENTERTAINMENT, our identity is rooted in power, wisdom, and an ancient legacy. The 'Ten Crowns' represent the ten heads of the great King Ravana—a symbol of supreme intellect, mastery over the arts, and an unmatched vision that spanned across horizons.

Just as the legend possessed ten different perspectives of wisdom, we approach cinema through ten essential lenses of creativity. Founded by Hansana Edirisinghe and Sayodya Hasaranga, we merge modern technology with the grandeur of our heritage. We don’t just tell stories, we rule the screen with the spirit of a king.

Our Vision

To be the ultimate throne of cinematic brilliance, where legendary heritage meets futuristic storytelling, redefining the standards of global filmmaking from the heart of our nation.

Our Mission

To craft regal visual experiences by mastering the ten essential perspectives of creativity. We are committed to empowering stories with profound wisdom, unmatched precision, and the spirit of a king.
