Born From Legend

At TEN CROWNS ENTERTAINMENT, our identity is rooted in power, wisdom, and an ancient legacy. The 'Ten Crowns' represent the ten heads of the great King Ravana—a symbol of supreme intellect and mastery over the arts.

Founded by Hansana Edirisinghe and Sayodya Hasaranga, we merge modern technology with the grandeur of our heritage. We don’t just tell stories, we rule the screen with the spirit of a king.

Our Vision

Our Mission
